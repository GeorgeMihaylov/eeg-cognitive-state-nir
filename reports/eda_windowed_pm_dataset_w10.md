## Interpretation

1. This EDA checks whether the 10-second windowed PM/POW dataset is suitable for baseline modeling.
2. `source` must be preserved because `gpn_data` and `Old_EEG` are different data domains.
3. Rows with missing `label_q5` or `target_main` should be excluded from the first supervised baseline.
4. Quantile labels are balanced by construction and should be treated as weak labels.
5. Strong source-level or subject-level distribution shifts should be considered during validation.
6. The first baseline should use GroupKFold/LOSO by `subject_id`, not only random split.
